DeepSeek Spend Monitor

Flash firmware to your ESP32-C3 Supermini — right from Chrome

1. Connect & Flash

  1. Plug in your ESP32-C3 Supermini via USB
  2. Hold BOOT (GPIO9), tap RST, release BOOT
  3. Click Connect below and select your serial port
  4. Click Install to flash the firmware

2. After Flashing

  1. Unplug and re-plug the device
  2. Connect to WiFi network "DeepSeekMonitor"
  3. Visit 192.168.4.1 to enter your WiFi & API key
